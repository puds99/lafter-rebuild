## FREE PARTNERSHIP TACTICS

- Send 100-500 micro-influencers free premium access
- No posting requirements (reduces pressure, increases authenticity)
- Focus on creators already discussing mental health/comedy
- Expected 13:1 ROI based on similar app campaigns

### 2. Beta Testing Program
- Invite comedians to test new features before public release
- Create exclusive "Comedy Creator Council" for feedback
- List them as official beta testers (social proof for them)
- They get bragging rights about shaping the app

### 3. Co-Creation Opportunities
- "Design Your Dream Feature" partnerships
- Name features after influential comedians who help develop them
- Create comedian-specific analytics dashboards
- Build custom integrations for their podcast/YouTube analytics

### 4. Affiliate-Style Program (No Upfront Cost)
- Unique referral codes for each creator
- They earn premium features for every X users they bring
- Gamify it: "Unlock the Laugh Master Badge" at 100 referrals
- Public leaderboard for competitive creators

### 5. Content Exchange Model
- Creators get exclusive app data for their content
- "According to Lafter.org, this joke has a 87% laugh rate"
- Weekly comedy metrics reports they can share
- Become their source for laughter statistics

### 6. Platform Partnerships (Free Tools)

- Stack Influence for product-only compensation
