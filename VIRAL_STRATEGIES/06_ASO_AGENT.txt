## SCREENSHOT OPTIMIZATION

### First Three Screenshots (Critical - 7 seconds to convert)
1. **Hero Shot**: Show main mood tracking interface with emotional benefit caption
   - Caption: "Track Your Emotional Journey"
   - Bright, welcoming colors with high contrast

2. **Feature Highlight**: Display key differentiator (humor/joy focus)
   - Caption: "Find Joy in Every Day"
   - Show unique Lafter features prominently

3. **Social Proof**: Community or achievement screen
   - Caption: "Join Thousands Finding Happiness"
   - Include stats or badges if available

### Design Best Practices
- Use emotion-driven visuals over perfect polish ("Ugly Ads" trend)
- Add short, benefit-driven captions (max 5 words)
- Localize screenshots for different markets
- A/B test first screenshot regularly (18% average improvement)
- Create Custom Product Pages for different user segments
- Platform-specific: iOS focuses on first 3, Android on first 1 + video

### Technical Requirements 2025
- Mandatory 6.9-inch iPhone size for iOS
- No longer need 5.5-inch screenshots
- High contrast and bright colors perform better
- Test captions, color schemes, and feature order separately

## APP PREVIEW VIDEO

### Video Strategy (20-40% conversion boost)
- **Length**: 15-30 seconds max (iOS), 30 seconds-2 minutes (Android)
- **First 5 seconds**: Show core value proposition immediately
- **Watch time reality**: Average 4-6.5 seconds, <20% completion rate
- **Design for silent**: Videos autoplay muted - use subtitles/captions

### Content Structure
1. Seconds 0-3: Problem identification (feeling stressed/anxious)
2. Seconds 3-8: Solution showcase (Lafter's mood tracking)
3. Seconds 8-12: Key features rapid montage
4. Seconds 12-15: Social proof/results

### Production Tips
- Show actual app UI, not mockups (Apple requirement)
- Test different opening scenes with Custom Product Pages
- Second video can drive 24% more installs
- Use authentic, raw footage over polished studio work
- Include emotional moments and user testimonials

## GETTING FEATURED

### Apple App Store Featuring Process
1. **Submit via App Store Connect**: Featuring Nominations dashboard
2. **Lead time**: Minimum 2 weeks, up to 3 months for wider featuring
3. **Review process**: Up to 30 days for response

### Key Requirements for Featuring
- **Rating**: Must be 4.5 stars or higher
- **Regular updates**: Shows active development and user care
- **Localization**: Multiple language support increases chances
- **New Apple features**: Implement latest iOS capabilities
- **Accessibility**: Support for diverse user needs
- **Innovation**: Unique approach to mental wellness

## REVIEW GENERATION

### Target Metrics
- **Minimum rating**: 4.5 stars (benchmark for 2025)
- **Response rate**: 90%+ to all reviews
- **Review velocity**: 10+ new reviews weekly

### Strategic Timing for Review Requests
1. After completing first week of mood tracking

- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours

- Identify user segments most likely to leave positive reviews
- Monitor for keywords in reviews (Google Play considers these for ranking)

## RISK MITIGATION

### Common ASO Mistakes to Avoid
- Don't keyword stuff (looks spammy, hurts conversion)
- Don't buy reviews (results in app removal)
- Don't ignore negative reviews (kills trust)
- Don't use misleading screenshots (increases uninstalls)
- Don't neglect localization (missing 60% of market)

### Platform Policy Compliance
- Never incentivize positive reviews directly
- Ensure all health claims are substantiated
- Include required disclaimers about not replacing therapy
- Respect user privacy in review responses
- Follow screenshot content guidelines strictly
